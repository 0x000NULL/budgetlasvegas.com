Our insurance VIP program at Go Rentals is all about personalized attention when renting cars to the clients of brokers, insurance carriers and insurance agencies.

We offer a personalized and unique experience when booking your clients' rental vehicles. Our team is changing the way rental cars are handled by streamlining the process and taking the headache out of making arrangements for your clients. We strive for excellence in all aspects of our business and yours.

◦ We are available seven days a week for your convenience.

◦ Our team consists of experienced insurance claims professionals.

◦ Servicing over 100 major cities.
